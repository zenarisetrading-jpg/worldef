Engineering

AI Full-Stack Engineer
(2–4 Years Experience)

Remote / Hybrid • Full-time

Job Summary

We are building an AI-powered Amazon advertising platform and are looking for a Full-Stack Engineer to help us build and scale core product features. You will work closely with founders and product leaders to ship AI-enabled workflows, APIs, and web interfaces used by advertisers and operators.

This is a hands-on role focused on building reliable, production-ready systems in a fast-moving startup environment.

Responsibilities

  • Build and ship product features across backend and frontend
  • Integrate AI/LLM APIs into keyword research, bid optimization, and analytics workflows
  • Develop and maintain scalable APIs and data pipelines
  • Build clean, responsive web interfaces
  • Support deployment, monitoring, and basic DevOps workflows
  • Implement authentication and basic access controls
  • Collaborate on system design and product decisions
  • Debug, optimize, and improve system performance

Required Skills & Experience

  • 2–4 years of experience in full-stack development
  • Strong proficiency in Python or Node.js
  • Experience building REST APIs
  • Working knowledge of PostgreSQL or similar databases
  • Experience with React, Next.js, or similar frameworks
  • Experience working with third-party APIs (AI/Cloud preferred)
  • Familiarity with Git, Docker, and basic cloud deployments
  • Good understanding of software engineering best practices

Nice to Have

  • Exposure to AI/LLM platforms (OpenAI, Claude, etc.)
  • Familiarity with Amazon Ads or e-commerce platforms
  • Experience with data pipelines or analytics systems
  • Knowledge of AWS/GCP/Azure
  • Startup or early-stage product experience

Why Join Us

  • Build a real AI-driven ad tech platform from early stage
  • High ownership and learning opportunity
  • Work closely with founders and product leadership
  • Opportunity to grow into senior/lead roles
