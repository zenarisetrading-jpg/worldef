How Counterfactual Analysis Works

We compare your actual results to a baseline: "What would have happened if you maintained old efficiency at the new spend level?"

  • Offensive Win: Spend increased + beat baseline → Efficient scaling
  • Defensive Win: Market shrank, but you beat expected drop → Good defense
  • Decision Gap: Spend increased but missed expectations → Learn & adjust
  • Market Drag: External headwinds (excluded from attribution)
