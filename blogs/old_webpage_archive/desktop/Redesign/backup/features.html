What is Decision Intelligence?

The Problem

Most PPC tools show you before/after metrics. But they can't tell you if your decisions caused the change, or if it was just market luck.

❌ Black Box Reporting

"Your ROAS went from 2.5 to 3.0 after our optimizations!"

(But the whole account went up 18%. Market trend? Seasonality? You'll never know.)

The Solution

AdPulse uses counterfactual analysis to calculate what would have happened without your changes—then measures the difference.

✓ White Box Reporting

"Expected $8,200 → Got $9,450 = +$1,250 Decision Impact"

This is the incremental revenue your decision added, adjusted for market baseline.

How It Works

1

Track Every Decision

When you change a bid, add a negative, or harvest a keyword—we log it with timestamp and details.

2

Measure at 14/30/60 Days

Amazon attribution is slow. We wait for real results, not 7-day noise.

3

Calculate Expected Outcome

Based on account-wide trends and 30-day rolling baselines, we model what would have happened without your change.

4

Show the Difference

Actual outcome - Expected outcome = Verified Decision Impact. No trust-me-bro claims.
